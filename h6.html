HTML Tags and Classes for School Website

  • <header>: For the website header.
  • <nav>: For the navigation menu.
  • <section>: To define content sections (e.g., subjects).
  • <article>: To display individual pieces of content (e.g., an assignment).
  • <footer>: For the footer containing school contact details.
  • <h1> to <h3>: For headings like "Exams" or "Assignments."
  • <p>: For paragraphs (e.g., descriptions of assignments).
  • <ul> and <li>: For lists, such as a list of subjects or teachers.
  • <a>: For links to other resources or pages.
  • <table>: To display timetables, exam schedules, etc.
  • <img>: For images like teacher photos or school logos.
  • <div>: To group and style elements.
